Our Story



Code for Change is a student-led organization dedicated to assisting emerging charities and non-profits in enhancing their online presence. Recognizing that one of the most significant challenges for newly established charities is navigating online marketing and establishing a digital footprint, Code for Change is committed to supporting these organizations in their early stages.
